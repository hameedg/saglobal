What we do


We are leading company in Turkmenistan, specializing on the export of commodities and fertilizers, with its further marketing on the global markets. Our company has offices and representatives in UAE, Turkey, Turkmenistan, India and Georgia. We have necessary experience and knowledge of logistics and various routes of delivery of local products from local plants, ports and stations to Black Sea, Middle East, South-East Asia, South America and other countries. We have solutions for packaging, wagons loading, and worked out all possible routes of delivery for any end-client. SA Global was incorporated in 2015, in UAE, and till today we have lifted more that 1.5 million metric tons of cargo from Turkmenistan and Uzbekistan, and marketed it to Middle East, Far East, South-East Asia and CIS countries. We always strive to achieve perfection in our job, and will make sure to deliver cargo to the end-buyer, compliant to highest international standards.
